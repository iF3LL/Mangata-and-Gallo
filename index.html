Who are we?

Mangata & Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings and anniversaries. We primarily operate online and and a small storefront in Austin, Texas with an atelier.
Our selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look.
Mariana, owner and Austin local, is well known for her jewelry designs. After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened a store in her hometown, Austin and started to grow her business online.
